×
  • Participants are allowed to explore their horizons of creativity in the respective engineering fields and have to provide an efficient solution for a particular Global Challenge.
  • Statements using 5W’s technique of Who, What, When, Where, and Why will be appreciated while defining the challenge.

Global Challenge:

1. A Million tons of surgical masks are thrown as garbage across different parts of the world. Develop possible techniques for the disposal of medical waste and used surgical masks.


2. Water scarcity is going to threaten our lives.Resolve methods to reduce it and management techniques to use the available resources more efficiently.


3. Look out for environmentally friendly ways and disposable methods to reduce E-waste.


4. Analyze possible techniques and alternative steps to manage and control Stubble burning.


5. Nurture the next generation by providing a better solution for “Ambient air” that ensures a healthy life stabilization.


6. Come up with an innovative idea to solve problems of water shortage, waste management, and the degrading environment which revolutionizes future farming

After the registration, event is followed by two rounds.

1. Participants need to submit abstracts in pdf format only.

Round-2: -

1. The presentation will be conducted through Google /Zoom meetings.

2. The presentation session will be followed by a Q&A interaction.

Cryptera'21

Cryptera’21 is a national-level virtual innovation and exhilarating competition organized by Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies-Nuzvid, one of India’s premier technical institutions. As a part of National Engineer’s Day, the institution launches CRYPTERA’21, a virtual technical competition to be held from SEPT 10 to 15, to foster creativity among students and motivate them to work towards excellence in the areas of technology. Participants are invited from leading technological and engineering institutes.

CRYPTERA is a series of live interactive virtual events planned to enhance and motivate young Indian minds and ignite competitive spirit despite being at home. The team has mapped everything we had on the ground to online space and participants are going to witness competitions and webinars conducted in the emerging and cutting-edge technologies in the respective fields of Engineering.

Aims to enlighten the students on the principles, working concepts, and providing solutions for real-life challenges. Overall, the CRYPTERA will be an incredible platform for the participants to get exposed to the latest technologies by evolving innovative solutions.

It is also an opportunity to meet up with peers from across the industry to discuss current approaches to upcoming technologies and an opportunity for interaction.

Series of Events are going to be conducted and for young ignited Engineers, CRYPTERA is like a Treasure of Technology which changes once perspective towards engineering.

Incentives:

1. E-Certificate will be provided.

2. Chance to be a part of the virtual fest and win amazing prizes.
